Rubik's Cube for Dummies

Moves Notation

1. White Cross

1A. Upside down: FF
1B. Elevator: F' U' R U

2. White Corners

2A. Elevator on the right: F D F'
2B. Elevator on the left: F' D' F
2C. Upside-down: R' DD R D
& repeat 2B

🔃 3. Middle Layer

3A. Insert right: U R U' R' U' F' U F
3B. Insert left: U' L' U L U F U' F'

4. Orient Yellow Cross

4A. Minus: F R U R' U' F'
4B. L Shape: F U R U' R' F'

5. Permute Yellow Edges

5A. Swap Neighbours: R UU R' U' R U' R' U'

6. Permute Last Layer

6A. Rotate 3: L' U R U' L U R' U'

7. Orient Last Layer

7A. Orient: L' UU L U L' U L R UU R' U' R U' R'
