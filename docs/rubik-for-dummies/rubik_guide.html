Rubik's Cube for Dummies

Move Notation

Face moves: Rotate the indicated face 90° clockwise (when looking at that face).

  • R - Right face
  • L - Left face
  • U - Up (top) face
  • D - Down (bottom) face
  • F - Front face
  • B - Back face
  • M - Middle vertical layer (between L and R, same direction as L)

Inverse moves: Add ' for 90° counter-clockwise (e.g., R').

Double moves: Add 2 for 180° rotation (e.g., U2).

1. White Cross

1A. Upside down: F2
1B. Elevator: D M D' M'

2. White Corners

2A. Elevator: D' R' D R
2B. Elevator mirrored: D F D' F'
2C. Upside-down: R' D2 R D
